Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our AI avatar platform

How realistic are the AI avatars?

Our AI avatars are designed to look highly realistic with natural facial expressions, lip movements, and gestures. While they are computer-generated, they provide a professional and engaging appearance that is suitable for various content types.

What languages are supported for speech?

Our platform currently supports over 20 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, Korean, Italian, and more. We're constantly adding new languages and improving existing ones.

How long does it take to generate a video?

Video generation typically takes between 30 seconds to 2 minutes, depending on the length of your text and the complexity of the animations. Our system processes videos in real-time and provides progress updates throughout the generation process.

What video formats and resolutions are available?

We provide videos in MP4 format with H.264 encoding, which is compatible with most platforms and devices. Our videos are available in 720p and 1080p resolutions, ensuring high quality while maintaining reasonable file sizes.

Can I customize the background of my avatar video?

Yes, you can select from a variety of pre-designed backgrounds or upload your own image to use as a background. We also offer a green screen option that allows you to easily integrate your avatar into other videos during post-production.

Do I own the videos created on your platform?

Yes, you retain full ownership of all videos created using our platform. You can use them for personal or commercial purposes in accordance with our terms of service. However, the avatar models themselves remain the property of our platform.
